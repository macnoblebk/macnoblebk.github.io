Common questions

How do notifications work?
Singlething sends two optional daily reminders — a morning nudge to set your intention and an evening nudge to log it — at the times you chose during setup. You can turn them off anytime in iOS Settings → Notifications → Singlething.

Where is my data stored?
Everything stays on your device. Singlething has no account and collects no data. See our Privacy Policy.

How do I change my reminder times?
Reminder times are chosen once during onboarding. In-app editing is planned for a future update — in the meantime you can disable notifications in iOS Settings.

How do I remove my data?
Deleting the app removes all of its data from your device.
