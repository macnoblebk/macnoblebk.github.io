Privacy Policy for Singlething

Effective date: June 30, 2026

Singlething ("the app") is designed to respect your privacy. This policy explains what information the app handles and how. In short: everything stays on your device, and we don't collect, transmit, or sell any of your data.

Information we collect

None. Singlething does not collect personal information. We have no servers, no accounts, and no analytics. We cannot see your intentions, your activity, or anything else you do in the app.

Information stored on your device

To function, the app saves the following locally on your device only:

  • The daily intentions you write and whether you logged or skipped them.
  • Your chosen reminder times and whether you've completed onboarding.

This information is stored on your device and is never sent to us or to any third party. It may be included in your device's encrypted backups (for example iCloud or Finder backups) if you have those enabled — that is controlled by your device settings, not by the app.

Notifications

If you grant permission, Singlething schedules local notifications to remind you to set and log your intention at the times you choose. These reminders are generated entirely on your device. You can disable them at any time in iOS Settings → Notifications → Singlething, and the app works without them.

Third parties

Singlething contains no third-party advertising, analytics, tracking SDKs, or social media frameworks. No data is shared with anyone.

Children

The app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Removing your data

Because all data lives on your device, deleting the app removes all of its data.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised effective date.
